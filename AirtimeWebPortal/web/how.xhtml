1. Enter your name
2. Enter your phone number
3. Enter your email
4. Confirm your email address

For registration on android you simply follow these steps

1. Enter your first name
2. Enter your last name
3. Enter your phone number
4. Enter your email
5. Enter your pin
6. Retype your pin
7. Confirm or cancel your registration
8. Redirection to your account

Registration Screen

For already registered people if they want to use the application they will need to login.

1. Provide a valid phone number
2. Provide a valid PIN

Login Screen

How to add funds (load your wallet)?

When you click on Add Airtime menu you are prompted to choose where you want your money be deducted from among available sources order to be loaded into your wallet. On the upper center it's your current balance

1. Choose from available payment method: Bank account, MoMo in order to be loaded into your wallet.
2. Type amount
3. Proceed

Add Amount Screen

How to send airtime to a single recipient?

Sending airtime to single recipient can be buying airtime for yourself or someone else. It is simply made up following steps:

1. Input the recipient number
2. Enter amount
3. Send

Send to single recipient Screen

How to send airtime to multiple recipients?

Sending airtime to multiple recipients is simply made up following steps:

1. Select from your contact list the desired numbers not greater than three recipients
2. specify amount for every recipient
3. send airtime

Send to multiple recipients Screen
